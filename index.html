Jellof Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of rice (any choice of rice)
  • 1/2 kilo of Chicken
  • 45ml of groundnut oil
  • 2 large chopped bell pepper
  • 5 red tomatoes
  • 2 sachet of gino tomatoes
  • 1 sachet of gino thyme
  • 1 sachet of gino curry
  • 4 pieces of bay leaves
  • 3 cubes of maggi
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 piece of ginger

Instructions

  1. Boil the meat half way to get the broth
  2. Airfry the meat for about 375 degree
  3. Wash the rice with hot water to remove the starch
  4. In a seperate pot heat up oil and fry the onion
  5. Add the tomatoe paste anf fry for about 5 minutes
  6. Add the chopped bell pepper and chopped fresh tomatoes
  7. Fry for about 8 minutes, then add the chicken broth
  8. Then add the curry, thyme, bay leaves, maggi and salt to your taste
  9. Once it start boiling add the rice, stir well and cover
  10. Keep checking back, once it's done, it's ready to be served
